About Nyay Vistar

Bridging the Gap Between Law & Technology

NyayVistaar is a technology-enabled legal services platform offering professional legal services in India and across international jurisdictions.

We work in collaboration with a global network of experienced lawyers and legal professionals to provide reliable, jurisdiction-specific legal support to individuals, startups, enterprises, and multinational businesses. With a strong foundation in Indian legal services, NyayVistaar supports clients with legal advisory, documentation, compliance, corporate, and commercial legal requirements. At the same time, our international legal network enables seamless access to global legal support, cross-border legal services, and coordination with international lawyers and law firms, ensuring accurate guidance across multiple jurisdictions.

From SARFAESI recovery for the largest NBFCs to Gram Nyayalaya tracking for rural legal aid — our platform speaks the language of Indian law, in Hindi and English.
